A donation of $20 can go a long way.

Pet Food

Your money goes straight to a pet food package that can last a homeless person and their pet for a month.

Pet Supplies

Your money goes straight to a pet supply package including grooming tools and toys that make sure the pets of the homeless are happy.

Veterinary Care

Your money goes to funding our vets to give emergency or regular consultations. Every 10 people that donate, a pet can get a vaccination.

Emergency Housing

Your money goes to homing the pets of the homeless AND the homeless for when they need it the most.
